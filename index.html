WELCOME TO ICTAZ-NIPA CHAPTER

The ICT Association of Zambia (ICTAZ) NIPA Chapter is a professional network of ICT enthusiasts and Students within the National Institute of Public Administration- NIPA community.

About Us

We are a NIPA student association under the Information and Communication Technology Association of Zambia (ICTAZ), aiming to register ICT professionals and regulate their conduct in the ICT sector.
ICTAZ collaborates with the Ministry of Technology and Science and other stakeholders to ensure laws and instruments are in place for professionalizing the ICT sector. Membership categories include Fellow, Full Member, Associate, Licentiate, Affiliates, Student, and Corporate Membership
